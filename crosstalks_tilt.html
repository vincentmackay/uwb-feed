This page contains crosstalk simulations of a dish with the next closest dish when both are gradually tilted by 10 degrees increments, from 0 to 30 degrees. I used a dish with an f/D=0.25, and the feeds don't have a can around them.

In that setup, the tilting is done around the axis perpendicular to the dish separation vector. In other words, one dish tilts towards the other while the other tilts away, as seen below:

The crosstalks decrease with tilt.

Note that in this case, the tilting axis is still parallel/perpendicular with the polarization planes, but the dish separation vector is at 45 degrees, as seen below:

Again, the crosstalks decrease with tilt.
